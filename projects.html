    Facebook

    Facebook is a popular social media platform founded by Mark Zuckerberg in 2004. It allows users to connect with friends and family, share photos and updates, and discover new interests. With over 2.8 billion monthly active users, Facebook has become one of the largest social networking sites in the world.

    Netflix

    Netflix is a streaming service that offers a wide variety of TV shows, movies, and documentaries to its subscribers. Founded in 1997 by Reed Hastings and Marc Randolph, Netflix has revolutionized the way people consume entertainment. Users can watch content on various devices, including smartphones, tablets, and smart TVs.

    SpaceX

    SpaceX, or Space Exploration Technologies Corp., is an American aerospace manufacturer and space transportation company founded by Elon Musk in 2002. SpaceX is known for developing the Falcon and Starship rockets and the Dragon spacecraft. The company has made significant advancements in reusable rocket technology, reducing the cost of space exploration and making it possible for humans to travel to Mars in the future.

    Google Maps

    Google Maps is a web mapping service developed by Google. It provides users with detailed maps, satellite imagery, street view, and real-time traffic conditions. Google Maps can be accessed on desktop computers as well as mobile devices through the app. It offers features like directions for driving, walking, and public transportation, making it a valuable tool for navigation and exploring places.
