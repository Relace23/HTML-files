Blog Post 1: The Importance of Web Accessibility

Web accessibility is crucial for ensuring that people with disabilities can use and interact with websites effectively. It involves designing and developing websites in a way that makes them accessible to all users, regardless of their abilities or disabilities. By implementing accessible design practices, we can create a more inclusive online environment for everyone.

Citation: Web Accessibility Initiative (WAI). (n.d.). Introduction to Web Accessibility. Retrieved from https://www.w3.org/WAI/WCAG21/quickref/

Blog Post 2: The Future of Artificial Intelligence

Artificial intelligence (AI) is advancing rapidly, with applications ranging from autonomous vehicles to healthcare diagnostics. As AI technology continues to evolve, it raises important ethical and societal questions. Understanding the potential benefits and challenges of AI is essential as we shape its future impact on various industries and society as a whole.

Citation: Russell, S., & Norvig, P. (2016). Artificial Intelligence: A Modern Approach. Pearson.
